About Jim

I'm a Data Scientist working at the Uppsala Monitoring Centre in Uppsala, Sweden. I work in the research department, where I will tackle various projects related to detecting and preventing adverse drug reactions.

Prior to this, I worked for 2 years in a research group at Klarna in Stockholm, Sweden, and I completed my PhD in Astrophysics in 2018. I worked on a wide range of topics in astronomy, machine learning and statistics.
